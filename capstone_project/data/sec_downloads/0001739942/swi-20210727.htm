Item 1.01
Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On July 27, 2021 (the “Amendment No. 5 Effective Date”), SolarWinds Holdings, Inc. (the “Borrower”), an indirect, wholly owned subsidiary of SolarWinds Corporation (the “Company”), entered into an amendment no. 5 (the “Amendment”) among the Borrower, SolarWinds Intermediate Holdings I, Inc., the guarantors identified therein and Credit Suisse AG, Cayman Islands Branch, as administrative agent, which amends that certain First Lien Credit Agreement, dated as of February 5, 2016, by and among the Borrower, SolarWinds Intermediate Holdings I, Inc., the other guarantors party thereto, Credit Suisse AG, Cayman Islands Branch, as administrative agent and collateral agent, Goldman Sachs Lending Partners LLC, Credit Suisse Securities (USA) LLC, Macquarie Capital (USA) Inc. and Nomura Securities International, Inc., as joint lead arrangers and joint bookrunners, Goldman Sachs Lending Partners LLC, as syndication agent, and Goldman Sachs Lending Partners LLC, as documentation agent (as amended, restated, amended and restated, supplemented or otherwise modified from time to time prior to the Fifth Amendment Effective Date, the “Credit Agreement”).

The Amendment amended the Credit Agreement to, among other things, extend the maturity date of the revolving credit facilities under the Credit Agreement to August 5, 2023.

The foregoing description of the Amendment is qualified in its entirety by reference to the Amendment, a copy of which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and incorporated herein by reference.

Item 8.01Other Events.

On July 30, 2021, the Board of Directors of the Company declared a special one-time cash dividend, to be paid following the effectiveness of, and after giving effect to, the two-for-one (2:1) reverse stock split to be effective at 5:00pm ET on July 30, 2021, equal to $1.50 per share of common stock issued and outstanding as of August 9, 2021, to be paid on August 24, 2021 (the “Special Dividend”). Without giving effect to the 2:1 reverse stock split, the Special Dividend would represent a dividend of approximately $0.75 per share of common stock. The Special Dividend will be funded primarily from cash distributed to the Company by N-able, Inc. (“N-able”) in connection with the separation and distribution of N-able from the Company that was completed on July 19, 2021.

On July 30, 2021, the Company issued a press release announcing the declaration of the Special Dividend. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference. Exhibit 99.1 is being furnished and shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liability of that section. The information in Exhibit 99.1 to this report shall not be incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act.
