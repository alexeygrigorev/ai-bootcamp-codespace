Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

Annual Meeting Results

On June 24, 2015, Yahoo! Inc. (the “Company”) held its 2015 Annual Meeting of Shareholders (the “Annual Meeting”). At the Annual Meeting, the Company’s shareholders voted on the election of directors and the other proposals described in the Company’s definitive proxy statement on Schedule 14A filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April 29, 2015. The number of votes cast for and against and the number of abstentions and broker non-votes with respect to each matter voted on are set forth below.
