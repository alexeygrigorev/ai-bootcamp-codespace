Item 7.01
Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
Capital One Financial Corporation (the “Company”) has posted the summary of its annual stress test results required by the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 on its website (http://www.capitalone.com). Under “About” choose “Investors” to access the Investor Center and then choose “Capital One Stress Test Results” to view the current summary. The disclosure includes stress test results for the Company but does not take into account any capital actions the Company may have requested under the Federal Reserve’s 2020 Comprehensive Capital Analysis and Review process (“CCAR”).

The furnishing of the Dodd-Frank Act Stress Test results by the Company should not be taken as an indication of the Company’s performance as a part of the Federal Reserve administered stress tests, and no positive or negative inference regarding the Company’s capital plan (including any potential capital actions) should be drawn from this information. The Federal Reserve has indicated that banks should publicly disclose their Stress Capital Buffer results after markets close on June 29, 2020.

Note: Information in this report furnished pursuant to Item 7.01 shall not be deemed to be “filed” for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that Section. This report will not be deemed an admission as to the materiality of any information in the report that is required to be disclosed solely by Regulation FD.
