Revision of the Financial Targets for the Imaging Products & Solutions Segment

(Tokyo, Japan – October 29, 2015) - Sony Corporation (“Sony”) today announced its revised financial targets for the Imaging Products & Solutions (“IP&S”) segment for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2018, due to a realignment of its business segments, as set out in the table below.
 
■      Financial targets for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2018
IP&S   As announced on
November 25, 2014
 As announced on
October 29, 2015
Segment
  Sales* 650~700 billion yen  680~730 billion yen 
   Operating income margin 7~9%  7~9% 
 
This revision is due to the realignment of Sony’s business segments from the third quarter of the fiscal year ending March 31, 2016, to reflect a change as of October 1, 2015 in the Corporate Executive Officer in charge of the  medical business.  In connection with this realignment, the operations of Sony’s medical business, which were included in All Other, will be included in the IP&S segment.
