3. Use of proceeds
 
Sony Corporation expects to receive net proceeds of approximately 300 billion yen from the Japanese Public Offering, the International Offering, the International Option and the Capital Increase by way of Third-Party Allotment (collectively, the “Global Offering of Common Stock”), after deducting estimated offering expenses payable by Sony Corporation. Sony Corporation intends to use approximately 188 billion yen of the net proceeds from the Global Offering of Common Stock to fund capital expenditures in the Devices segment and the remainder to fund research and development expenditures in the Devices segment.
 
For further details on the use of proceeds, see the release titled “Issuance of New Shares, Secondary Offering of Shares to Cover Over-allotments, and Issuance of Convertible Bonds with Stock Acquisition Rights” announced on June 30, 2015.
