Advisory Vote on Executive Compensation.  At the meeting, the stockholders approved, on an advisory basis, the compensation of the Company’s named executive officers, with 90,495,427 votes for, 2,836,776 votes against, 222,179 votes abstaining and 9,208,951 broker non-votes.

 

Ratification of Independent Public Accountants.  At the meeting, the stockholders of the Company also voted to ratify the Audit Committee’s selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the Company’s independent registered public accounting firm for 2019, with 101,672,111 votes for, 754,214 votes against, and 337,008 votes abstaining.
