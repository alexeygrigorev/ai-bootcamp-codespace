Item 7.01
Regulation FD Disclosure.

On January 18, 2018, a subsidiary of Marriott International, Inc. (“Marriott”) sold the 740-room Sheraton Buenos Aires Hotel & Convention Center and the 180-room Park Tower, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Buenos Aires, and received cash of approximately $100 million. Marriott retains long-term management agreements for both properties located in downtown Buenos Aires, Argentina. Both of the hotels will undergo renovations of their guest rooms, meeting space, lobby and other public areas.
