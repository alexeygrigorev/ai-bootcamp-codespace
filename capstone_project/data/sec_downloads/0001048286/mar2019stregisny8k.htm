Item 7.01
Regulation FD Disclosure.
On October 31, 2019, a subsidiary of Marriott International, Inc. (“Marriott”) sold the St. Regis New York Hotel for a price of $310 million to Qatar Investment Authority (“QIA”). Marriott will continue to operate the hotel under a long-term management agreement with QIA. Located on prestigious Fifth Avenue in Manhattan, the property is comprised of 229 guest rooms and seven residential suite units that account for nine additional rooms.
